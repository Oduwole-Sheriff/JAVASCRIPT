WE ARE NOT HIRING

Disclaimer: Max Air is not currently recruiting for any positions, and we do not collect any fees from job seekers for employment opportunities.

Please be aware that scammers and fraudsters may falsely claim to represent Max Air and offer employment opportunities in exchange for a fee. We do not endorse or recommend any third-party recruitment agencies or their services. If you receive any unsolicited emails or calls from anyone claiming to be a representative of Max Air offering employment opportunities in exchange for a fee, please do not respond and report it to us immediately.

We encourage all job seekers to be cautious and to verify the authenticity of any employment opportunity before providing any personal or financial information. Max Air assumes no liability for any losses or damages that may result from responding to unsolicited emails or calls from anyone claiming to represent Max Air or offering employment opportunities on our behalf

The general public is advised to be guided, please.
